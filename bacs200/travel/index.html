Great Wall of China

Beijing is only an hour away from the place without a doubt One of the most famous historical buildings in the country: the great Chinese city wall. At Badaling Pass, the first part of the Great Wall Open to tourists in the 1950s, you can walk along the impressive Part of the Great Wall, dating back to the 16th century, as high as eight Meters high.

Tiananmen Square

Tiananmen Square (Tiananmen Square) It is the largest downtown square in the world. Designed to keep Ten thousand people, it was built to celebrate the tenth anniversary The Republic of China in 1958. Considered the center of communist China, The symbolism of the square can be traced back to May 4, 1919, when Students protested against the Chinese clause of the Treaty of Versailles
