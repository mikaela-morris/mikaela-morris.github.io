Atlantis

Antlantis Paradise Island is an ocean-themed resort in the Bahamas. An extravagant place with stunning views and plenty of activities to encounter. It features a variety of accommodations built around Aquaventure (the world-know waterpark). Fresh and saltwater lagoons, pools, marine habitats, water slides, and river rides are many most will experience during their time at Atlantis.

Goulding Cay Reefs

Goulding Cay Reef is a shallow coral reef located only 30 minutes from downtown Nassau. This Bahamiam coral reef consists mostly of elkhorn coral and thousands of rainbow-colored tropical fish of all shapes and sizes. Being that this reef is so shallow, seeing the beauty underwater world and not having to go very deep, Goulding Cay is the perfet reef for an entire family. It has also been named one of the top shallow water snorkeling locations by USA Today.

Versailles Gardens of The Bahamas

The Versailles Garden, located in Paradise Island, is one of the most luscious, structured, and truly historic in the Bahamas. It overseas the Nassau Harbor nearby, has multiple platforms/terraces, and its structure is about 75 feet wide. Monuments of great men in history, such as Napoleon, Hercules, and Franklin D. Roosevelt (32nd President of the United States) also stand here.
