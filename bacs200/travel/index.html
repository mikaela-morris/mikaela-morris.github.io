Atlantis

Extravagant place and stunning views. Go for a swim or if you're feeling adventurous, slide down the most famous water slide in Nassau. The slide is located in the Aquaventure water park and one of the most highly visited attraction by tourists.

Snorkel in the Coral Reefs

Snorkel through the beautiful coral reefs and explore the different species. Once in a lifetime opportunity to dive through the clear blue waters in Paradise Island.

Nassau Cruise Port

Because the Bahamas is a highly visited tourist destination, they have a highly recognized cruise port. This is where cruise ships park and local tourists can sometimes tour these ships, depending on the local regulations.
