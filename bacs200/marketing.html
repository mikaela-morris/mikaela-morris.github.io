Welcome to Amy Morris Swim School!!

"Swim Hard, Play Hard!"

Amy Morris Swim School is a Spring & Summer swim school offering private swim instruction. Aside from opening her own swim school in 2002, Amy swam at the National and Collegiate levels. Upon graduating from college, Amy developed a love for little ones and wished to carry on her passion for the sport of swimming. AMSS specializes in teaching infants and toddlers water safety and educating their family of its importance. AMSS offers the most efficient swim instruction in the central valley of California. How? Owner, Amy and her staff, do this by teaching with a balance of love and discipline.

Below are a few key aspects of what sets Amy Morris Swim School apart from other swim schools in the valley:

  • Creating a personal bond and building trust with the child and their family
  • Enthusiastic environment creates a fun atmosphere for the child
  • Teacher centered lessons and private instruction
  • Achievments are acknowledged with a gold medal upon completion of lessons
  • Instructors are highly trained by Amy (owner)
  • Instructors come to AMSS with a passion for children, teaching, and the sport of swimming

Remember, to become a "safe swimmer" is our absolute minimum goal. Our hope is to help every swimmer build confidence, respect, and love for the water. See you at the pool!
